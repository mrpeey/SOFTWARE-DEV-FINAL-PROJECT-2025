Digital Literacy Resources

Enhance your digital skills with our curated collection of resources

Why Digital Literacy Matters

In today's digital world, having basic computer and internet skills is essential for education, employment, and daily life. Our digital literacy program helps community members develop these crucial skills through accessible resources and hands-on learning opportunities.

Learning Areas

Basic Computer Skills

Learn to use a computer, navigate operating systems, and manage files.

Internet & Web Browsing

Master web browsing, search engines, and online safety practices.

Document Creation

Create and edit documents, presentations, and spreadsheets.

Digital Security

Protect yourself online with privacy settings and security practices.

Mobile Devices

Use smartphones and tablets effectively for communication and learning.
